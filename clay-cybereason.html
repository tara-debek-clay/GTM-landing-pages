How Clay works for Cybereason

Three steps from raw data to booked meetings.

1
Unify & Enrich
Pull your CRM accounts, partner lists, and inbound leads into Clay. Instantly enrich every record with 100+ data sources—tech stack (are they running CrowdStrike or SentinelOne?), employee headcount in IT/security, recent breach events, regulatory exposure (HIPAA, CMMC, NIS2), and open security engineering roles that signal an active evaluation cycle.
2
Score & Orchestrate
Apply custom scoring models that weight signals specific to Cybereason's ICP: companies with legacy AV still deployed, those who've had a public incident in the past 12 months, enterprises expanding to hybrid cloud, or MSSPs actively adding MDR services. Automatically route scored accounts to the right rep, partner, or sequence.
3
Activate & Measure
Sync enriched, scored accounts directly to Salesforce, HubSpot, or your sequencing tools (Outreach, Salesloft). Trigger personalized email campaigns that reference each prospect's actual security posture. Measure which data signals and sequences drive the most demos booked, and double down on what converts.
