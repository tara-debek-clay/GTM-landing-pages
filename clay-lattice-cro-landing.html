How It Works

From cold list to booked meeting — automated

Clay connects your data sources, enriches every account automatically, and feeds your reps a prioritized, personalized pipeline — without manual lift.

Step 01
Unify & Enrich

Pull your target accounts from your CRM, intent platforms, or a CSV. Clay enriches every row with 100+ data sources — company headcount, HR tech stack, org changes, open roles, and more — surfacing which accounts are actually in-market for Lattice right now.

Step 02
Score & Orchestrate

Apply AI-powered fit and timing scores to route accounts to the right rep, sequence, or nurture track. No manual triage. Reps see a daily queue of warm, enriched accounts ranked by revenue potential — not a flat list of 500 contacts to sort through.

Step 03
Activate & Measure

Push enriched, personalized sequences into Outreach, Salesloft, or HubSpot automatically. Track which signals drove meetings, which messages converted, and refine your playbook — so every quarter your outbound gets smarter, not just louder.
