How It Works

Three steps from signal to pipeline

Clay sits at the center of Liferay's GTM engine — enriching accounts, scoring intent, and triggering the right actions automatically.

1

Unify & Enrich

Pull in your CRM accounts and ICP target list, then waterfall 50+ data sources to enrich every record with tech stack, headcount, portal technology, compliance signals, and decision-maker contacts — without manual research. Perfect for Liferay's complex enterprise target accounts across financial services, healthcare, and manufacturing.

2

Score & Orchestrate

Apply AI-driven scoring rules specific to Liferay's ICP — e.g., organizations running legacy portals, enterprises adding self-service capabilities, or IT leaders recently hired. Route high-fit accounts to AEs for immediate outreach, mid-fit to automated sequences, and low-fit to nurture. No more spreading your team thin across the wrong accounts.

3

Activate & Measure

Push enriched, scored contacts directly into Salesforce, HubSpot, or Salesloft with personalized messaging built from real account signals. Track which signals and sequences are driving meetings and closed-won deals — so Liferay's GTM team can double down on what works and iterate fast.
