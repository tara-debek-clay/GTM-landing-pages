How It Works

From raw data to closed procedures — in three steps

Clay sits between your data sources and your CRM, automating the enrichment, scoring, and activation your team needs to grow.

1

Unify & Enrich

Pull NPI records, practice profiles, ASC affiliations, conference speaker data, and clinical publication history into a single enriched account view. No more tab-switching before territory calls.

2

Score & Orchestrate

Build custom scoring models that weight surgeon procedure volume, practice size, competitive device usage, and buying signals. Automatically tier accounts — from new surgeon targets to expansion accounts ready for iDose TR or iLink adoption.

3

Activate & Measure

Sync enriched, scored records directly to Salesforce with the context reps need to personalize outreach by specialty, procedure mix, and practice type. Track adoption velocity by territory and close the loop between marketing and field sales.
